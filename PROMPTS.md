# Background Image Prompts

All backgrounds were generated with **ChatGPT (image generation)** on March 18, 2026.

These images are used as fixed full-viewport backgrounds behind the game UI. They are darker and more atmospheric than the thumbnails, with visual weight pushed to the edges so white UI text remains readable over the dark center.

---

## Shared Background Style Directive

> Wide-angle digital matte painting, atmospheric and cinematic. Deep rich colors with heavy vignette darkening toward the center and top third. The scene should feel like ambient environmental art — more mood than detail. Soft painterly textures with film grain. Volumetric haze and warm rim lighting from below. The center must remain dark enough (near-black) to serve as a backdrop for white UI text. No text, no logos, no UI elements, no clearly readable card faces. Ultrawide 21:9 aspect ratio.
